- (decision) Resolved (own call, [ASSUMPTION]): Market Research stays synchronous by splitting into two blocking calls, not one-shot -- POST /market-research runs search->DAG->Validation and returns inline; if no edges escalated it continues straight into ATE and returns the full result in that same call (status: done). PATCH /market-research/{session_id}/edges submits per-edge decisions and, in that same blocking call, runs ATE Estimation and returns the final result. This keeps Market Research entirely request/response (matches user's sync choice) while still enforcing the Expert Validation hard barrier from AD-6's origin -- it just does so via two sequential sync calls instead of async job polling.
